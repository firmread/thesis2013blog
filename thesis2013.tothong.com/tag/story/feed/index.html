The idea now shift a bit, rather than have two separated chapter on childhood and grownup part. We decided to goes with the idea that the whole game happened in a week and in each night both player would dream about their childhood memories. Where the decision in the dream and things they do on each day will affect the event that happen later on.

Character background

a grown up boy
A salaryman who works day to day in not-so-enjoyable jobs who likes to hang out with friends in the city.

a grown up girl
A freelance hacker who working at home and enjoy her online relationship.
(her character design could inspired from a manga character, Arale.)

Opening scene

At the elevator hall, two facing elevators stops at the floor at the same time. As the crowd move their way out, a person left on each elevator. Their eyes meet while the elevator door slowly closing. The picture then cut to pitch black before the elevator closed

Then the story started at a week before this situation (might stated something like 7 days ago, or maybe not at all.)

Day 1 : Thursday

a boy 

  • waking up late in the morning
  • bus chasing minigame
    • miss the bus > late for job, boss advantage point -1
    • catch the bus > barely get to office right on time
  • boss argue mini game 1
    bad boss just blame you on anything anyway

    • win argument > boss advantage point +1
    • lose argument > boss advantage point -1
  • such a tough and terrible job he have

a girl

  • exercising at home on a running lane, and chatting online simultaneously (run+chat mini game)
    • fail to run fast enough > (?)
    • fail to make a good conversation > (?)
  • online chatting with her online date, providing some background on their relationship
a dream
  • water particles + rainbow mini game [futile]
  • first time they know each other is in their garden at home. They were watering their plants. And when their water joined rainbow suddenly appear. They look each other in the eyes and smile…
