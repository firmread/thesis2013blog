welcome – thatThey http://thesis2013.tothong.com Collaboration between Paul Cheng and Firm Tothong for MFADT thesis 2013 Mon, 08 Apr 2013 08:51:38 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.4.2 Welcome! http://thesis2013.tothong.com/welcome/ Fri, 16 Nov 2012 05:30:06 +0000 http://thesis2013.tothong.com/?p=12 “Portable Affection (working title)” is an interactive narrative game, aiming to release on iPad. The game need two players in which they will craft a story together through forgotten pieces of childhood memories.

The game create in collaboration between Paul Cheng and Firm Tothong as part of MFA thesis in Design and Technology at Parsons the New School For Design. With consultant of Colleen Macklin and Barbara Morris.

iPad is the first device that open up the market for digital tablet in 2010. The tablet is designed to fit the size of one hand interaction and to be personalized for one user. We are exploring the potential of the device as relationship facilitator and narrative tool through the ritual of games.
