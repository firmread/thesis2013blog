Originating as an investigation of the relationships between participation and spectatorship, ‘that they’ explores how two players’ negotiation emerge when the game required them to interface through the same device. And the choice they made in game also affects each other’s narrative direction.There is a long history of investigation of immateriality and participation in contemporary arts, as well as emerging new genre of interaction design.
